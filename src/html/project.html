Project

This website is made as part of the examination of a frontend development course taken at the Mid University of Sweden. The creator responisble for the site is Moa Berglund.

The base of the website is based on information retrieved from two API:s, one is from the Nobel Prize and one is from Google Books. The two API:s communicate with each other to present the data shown on the homepage of the website.

To get to know these API:s further you can visit:
The Nobel Prize Developer Zone
